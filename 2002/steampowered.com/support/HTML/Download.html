Instructions for Downloading Steam

You will first need to visit one of the download sites listed below and run the Setup.exe. This program contains the client executable for running Steam. You can find the setup direction at http://steamsupport.valvesoftware.com/Docs/Directions.html.

         http://www.redphive.org/files/steam/steam_v1.exe
         http://gpfiles.dhs.org/valve/steam_v1.exe
         http://mirror.nuclearfallout.net:113/steam_v1.exe
         http://www.ausgamers.com/steam_v1.exe

Steam automatically updates your PC clock if it is not accurate for your time zone. If you receive a message stating this, you will need to restart the Steam application.

When you first start Steam click on 'Create Account'. The 'Special Offer Key' you received in this email should go in the 'Special Offer Key' box in the 'Create Account' window. It should be 8 characters, is case-sensitive, and may contain odd characters. The 'Email Address' is case-sensitive. Please check that you are entering your email address exactly as it appears in the body of the email we sent to you.
