Step By Step :: Install Instructions

Instructions for Installing Steam - Directions for setting up a Steam account 

Step 1)  Go to the site included in your invite email and run the setup.exe by double clicking
             on the icon.

Step 2)  Choose "Run this program from its current location" and click "Ok".

Step 3)  Click "Yes" on the security warning window.

Step 4)  Choose the directory where you would like to install steam.

Step 5)  First time users click on the “Create Account” button.  Existing users enter your
             User Name (email address) and Password created when you set up the account
            then click “Log In”.


... and, for First-time Users

Step 6)  Please check that you have read the Valve license agreement and click the
            “I Agree” button.

Step 7)  Enter your E-mail Address, Password, and Special Offer Key on the registration
             screen and click the “finish” button.  Your Special Offer Key is an eight digit
             alphanumeric sequence that was generated based on the e-mail address you
             supplied for the beta.  The Special Offer Key was sent to the e-mail supplied.

You can choose any password.  This will be your way to login to your account for future access.  You will not be require to reenter the Special Offer Key again unless we make a major change to the system during beta that requires you to delete your account.  Please retain your Special Offer Key until the end of the beta.   

 

Step 8) The main user interface should appear.  Subscribe to the game(s) of your choice and
            follow the directions.   

Step 9) Pick a game to play on the “My Games” page.  If you “right mouse click” on the
           game icon you will have the options to start the game in full screen, widowed mode or
           start a dedicated game server.
