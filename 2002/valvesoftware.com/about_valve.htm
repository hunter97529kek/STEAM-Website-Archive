about Valve


Valve is a computer game development company founded by Gabe Newell and Mike Harrington and based in Kirkland Washington.

Valve's first product, Half-Life, has just been released by Sierra Studios.

Watch for Valve's Team Fortress, the ultimate team-based online action game, which will hit store shelves in the first part of 1999.
