employment opportunities

Our beliefs about work environments are pretty simple - give talented and experienced people the tools they need, the colleagues who will stimulate them, and the opportunity to build games they want to play. The hard part is finding the right people who have the professionalism, maturity, and talent to deliver in that kind of environment.

We've had to go pretty far afield in our search, and even after aggressive screening have averaged about 5 on-site interviews for each person we've hired.

If you have read through our corporate bios and think Valve sounds like the place for you, feel free to contact us even if you don't see a specific job listing that matches your background.

Senior Software Engineer

 

Successful candidates will have a bachelor’s degree in engineering or computer science and at least 5 years experience developing with C++ in a disciplined engineering environment.  Skills in database engineering, e-commerce, credit card transaction-based billing systems, and a desire to build complex systems are a must.

 

 

Project Manager Multimedia Design

 

Direct and manage computer game content development; Create functional and architectural specifications for game content; Use both internal and third-party 3-d modeling and dev tools to create game content according to functional and architectural specifications; Provide creative direction to a group of modelers, animators, level designers, texture artists, programmers and sound engineers; define milestones and meet project schedules; perform testing and validation consistent with functional and design specs and internal quality metrics; participate in public relations activities.

 

Requires bachelor’s degree in fine arts, computer science, or business(or equivalent), and 6 months of  experience  managing computer game development from conception and development through product shipment. Expertise using 3D modeling and development tools to create game levels; Proven track record creating high quality multiplayer game levels; Project management experience may be gained in an academic or commercial setting and may be concurrent with education.  Full-time position.  Competitive and benefits.

 

 

Game Development SDK Developer

 

A successful candidate will have at least 3 years programming experience in C and C++, and excellent written and verbal skills.  Game programming experience a plus but not required.  Responsibilities include working directly with game developers to design, port and enhance their games for Valve’s broadband delivery system and develop the SDK used by game developers.  This position may require some travel.
