infodecal
Half-Life entity

description

    The infodecal entity allows you to place special decals in your map such as scorch marks, blood aplatters, writing, etc.

usage

    You can place an infodecal entity as you'd place a normal entity, but Worldcraft also provides you with the infodecal placement tool.

    First, position the camera so that the 3D view displays the area you want to place the decal in. Now, make sure that the Selection and Decal tools are enabled. Where the texture groups are listed, select the decals.wad texture, then click on the Browse button.

      Note: The decal must be from decals.wad.

    Once you have the decal you want selected, click with the decal pointer on the 3D view. You will see the decal placed on a surface there. Click on the decal tool again to toggle it off, and you will then be able to select and position the infodecal in the 2D views.

      Note: The infodecal will reflect the texture properties of the brush it is placed on. To scale or flip the decal, change the properties of the surface it lies on. These changes won't be reflected in the Worldcraft 3D view, but they will be shown in the game. The decalflip.zip example map demonstrates this.
