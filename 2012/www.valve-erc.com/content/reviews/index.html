Below you will find reviews of completed maps. Review ratings consist of 3 marks, each out of 10. The Visual mark refers to the architecture and textures. The Ambient mark refers to lighting and sound. The Gameplay mark refers to the actual gameplay of the map. For information on submitting maps for review, look here.
