CS_grocery
by Gentoo Penguin
review by Graham 'Gonnas' Smith

INFORMATION
gametype: CS Hostage Rescue
number of players: 4 vs 4
download: cs_grocery.zip

CS_Grocery is brought to us by Gentoo Penguin and is currently in beta. This means that it’s allowed to have gameplay issues and maybe some bugs but the basic bones of the final map should be there. Unfortunately in this case, they're not.

Straight off I’ll jump in and comment on the most important area of any map, and the biggest flaw in this one. It’s just not fun! There are no areas in the map where enjoyable stand-offs take place or any tense fire fights. The layout and design leave the game open for a slow, boring game full of campers. Although I’ll admit there were only 5 of us, never was there a round that at the end we were looking forward to the next. Hostages were badly positioned, with one lonely hossie to far away from his friends. This made T’s reluctant to defend them, and CT’s even more reluctant to save them. CS always amazes me that no one actually completes the objective, it’s like playing CTF where no one caps the flag.

Gameplay isn’t the only problem, however. Visuals aren’t fantastic either. The custom textures that are included, although a welcome change from the norm, lack detail and are almost cartoonish in comparison to the standard CS ones. The skybox in particular was dreadful, custom or not it was plain to see where the corners met. Architecture isn’t bad, but definitely isn’t fantastic. From memory I can’t remember any curved areas, only a lot of boxes. There was a typical inexperienced mapper fault on show here as well, scaling. It’s not a problem throughout the entire map, but one of the rooms you walk into and are suddenly confronted with the mother of all fridges.

Ambience was better than the other two aspects of the map, but could still have been better. The darker areas could have been helped by adding some light patches and by brightening the camper spots. While the lighter places could have used some darker areas to increase atmosphere. Unfortunately no custom sounds featured in the map, but not many maps have them anyway so it’s unfair to prosecute this one for not.

What needs changing before final release:

  • Edit conflict areas and improve layout
  • Re-position Hossies for easier access
  • Add more detailed textures
  • Add light effects and change brightness levels

FINAL WORD
Numerous faults means Pengoo will have a lot of work to get this map up to a decent standard. Download anyway and give some feedback to help improve his skills as he definitely has some, I’ve seen a lot worse.

RATING: 4.0
visual: 5
ambient: 4
gameplay: 3
All ratings out of 10.0
