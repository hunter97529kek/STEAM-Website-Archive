3battlegrounds
by Benoit Champagne
review by Chris 'Jabyaeye' Roys

INFORMATION
gametype: HLDM
number of players: 4-12
download: 3battlegrounds

The 3 seperate areas of gameplay can exist in this map at the same time. The map is quite spread out with many opportunites.

The first area is wide open, sandy, and is surrounded by a boxy square cliff. Towards the middle is a large, rather empty house-like structure with a small sinkhole out front, and two round towers of rock nearby.

The second area is built more like something seen in the original Half-Life. It has a lab-like area with ladders, lifts, stairs, and assorted crates. This is where the entrance to the third area comes in. The main floor contains a Stargate-like teleportation system which takes you to the third area.

The third area has a Xen-like feel. It is wrapped in a thick layer of alien textures and sounds. Complete with moving and spinning parts, this area is the most eye pleasing.

PROS:
clean texture alignment and use
appropiate lighting
well constructed buildings
good weapon placement and abundancy

CONS:
no tying theme for the three seperate areas
canyon and Xen-like areas are too squared
there is no cover (crates or outcroppings) in open areas or long hallways

FINAL WORD
My final thoughts on this level are that it is somewhat lackluster in some areas, with thoughts of grandeur in other areas. It was really good in places and boring in others. I ended up giving it overall an average score.

RATING: 5.3
visual: 6
ambient: 5
gameplay: 5
All ratings out of 10.0
