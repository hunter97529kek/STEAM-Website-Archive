Beta Map Reviews
Reviews of maps in various stages of not being done.

Below you will find reviews of incomplete maps. The purpose of these reviews is to provide feedback to the map authors, as sort of a widescale beta test. If you download and look at any of the maps listed here, consider taking the time to email the author of the map with some constructive criticism or praise. For information on submitting maps for review, look here.
