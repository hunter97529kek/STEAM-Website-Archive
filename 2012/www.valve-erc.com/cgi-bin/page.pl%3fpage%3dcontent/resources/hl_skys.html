Using Half-Life Skies in Worldcraft
To make different skies for Half-Life in Worldcraft, you use the one generic texture named SKY wherever you want the sky to appear, then you enter the actual sky name as the "environment map (cl_skyname)" value in the Map Properties (accessible through the Map menu). Below are the valid sky names and their corresponding lateral textures (the top and bottom texture of the skybox has been left out to make for easier formatting).

Due to the high graphical content of this page, its load time may be lengthy.
